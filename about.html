About Stackly

We are engineering a cleaner, brighter future by deploying scalable renewable energy solutions worldwide. Our commitment is to power progress without compromising the planet.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by a team of climate scientists and software engineers, Stackly was born from the idea that technology and sustainability must converge to solve the global energy crisis. We started as a small R&D lab focused on optimizing solar grid integration.


Over the years, we expanded our focus to include utility-scale battery storage and proprietary smart grid management software. Today, Stackly is recognized as a leader in comprehensive clean energy infrastructure, serving communities and corporations across five continents.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish a decentralized, 100% renewable energy infrastructure that makes carbon-free power universally accessible and affordable.

Our Mission

To design, deploy, and manage the most efficient and reliable clean energy systems, accelerating the global transition to sustainability.
