Our Story: Sustainable Roots

Innovation Meets Responsibility

Founded in 2018 by a team of engineers and environmental scientists, Stackly was born from the realization that scalable, reliable green energy required a tech-first approach. We started small, optimizing local solar farms, and have since grown into a global provider of integrated energy management solutions.

Our commitment extends beyond generation. We focus on efficiency, grid resilience, and community engagement, ensuring that every project delivers long-term economic and ecological benefit.
