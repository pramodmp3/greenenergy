Frequently Asked Questions

Green energy systems, like solar panels, significantly reduce or eliminate your monthly electricity bills. Coupled with federal and local incentives, the initial investment often leads to substantial long-term savings and increased property value.

Most modern solar panels are designed to last 25 to 30 years or more. Stackly uses panels with a 25-year performance warranty, ensuring your system continues to generate power efficiently for decades.

Solar and wind systems alone typically do not work during an outage (due to safety regulations). However, when paired with an energy storage battery, your home can seamlessly switch to stored power, maintaining essential services during grid failures.

The timeline varies by project size, but generally, the physical installation takes 1 to 3 days. The entire process, including permits, inspection, and final connection to the grid, usually takes 4 to 8 weeks.
